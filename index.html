Projects

BearBucks

Under the guidance of CalHacks, an organization on campus, I am currently working with a team to develop BearBucks, a platform to help students and student-led organizations keep track of their finances. Our platform is unique in that it enables individuals to understand their finances using automated graphics while giving organizations the ability to seamlessly collaborate on their financial details and budgets.

CS:GO Data Analysis

As part of my IB Math HL research paper on probability, I developed a number of programs in Java designed specifically for data analysis on a database of over 300,000 rounds of Counter Strike: Global Offensive. I was able to leverage my knowledge of Java to quickly and seamlessly generate realistic probabilistic functions and integrate various kernel density estimations.
